Research Focus

I am a Lead Algorithm Engineer working at the intersection of 3D Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Deep Learning.

My work focuses on recovering and generating 3D structure from 2D observations, including geometry reconstruction, material reflectance modeling (BRDF), and generative 3D modeling.

I am particularly interested in differentiable rendering pipelines and large-scale synthetic data generation for training modern deep learning systems in 3D understanding tasks.

Multi-bounce Reflectometry
Developed a differentiable rendering framework for physically-based rendering (PBR) targeting inverse rendering tasks, including BRDF estimation and material recovery for real-world objects. The approach extends to concave geometries and improves the modeling of multi-bounce light transport in complex scenes.
Synthetic Data Generation
Pipeline for generating large-scale synthetic datasets with controllable assets and domain randomization.
